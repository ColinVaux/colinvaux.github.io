Past

I grew up in Lilburn, Ga, which is roughly 30 mins outside of Atlanta. I have had a bit of a wayward life path so far. For most of high school I worked at a movie theater and with the money I was able to save up went to New York for two weeks before starting college. I was in college for two semesters before dropping out, I have work a variety of different job in the time since then. I've worked in manufacturing gaining experience running an industrial printer and a CNC machine. I've worked in the service industry drawing on my ability to provide quality customer service. I've done solely food delivery as my income source requiring self discipline/time management. I've work in the eye care industry as an ophthalmic laboratory technician, running the machine which would edge the lens for glasses, then mounting the lenses into the glasses frame.
